Free Flight Fantasies

Thanks for stopping by to visit my hangar. When not working, I spend much of my free time designing, building and flying miniature aircraft. Sometimes they are Radio Controlled models, occasionally Control Line, but recently they are more likely smaller and lighter Free Flight models that are on their own once released to the winds. There is also a full scale airplane in my life.
