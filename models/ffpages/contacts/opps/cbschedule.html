RULES

  • Must have 3 flyers posting official times to give points for a class.
  • Points are awarded according to the number of official entries. eg. 10 entries First place gets 10 points, Second 9 points etc. down to Tenth place getting 1 point.
  • All events except Salem 6 and Jetco Blatter follow the official AMA or FAC rules.
  • Jetco ROG and Blatter 40 will be flown as the same class, times can be recorded with either or both types of plane in the same contest.
  • Jetco Blatter and Salem 6 must be built according to plans as to all dimensions and wood sizes.
  • Unlimited attempts to record the two best times which will be added together to determine placing in Jetco Blatter and Salem 6
