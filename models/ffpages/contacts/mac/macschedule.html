1998 Contest Schedule

In 1998, we will have one specified primary competitive event each month. Addionally, any other class can be contested based upon interest.

MonthDateEvent
January 25* Pussycat
February 22* Peanut
March 22 A-6
April 26 No-Cal
May 24 Phantom Flash
June 28 Delta Dart
July 26 Bostonian
August 23 "Target Time"
September 27 Biplane ***
October 18** Akro
November 22 No-Cal
December 27 Delta Dart

* - January and February Sessions will be flown in the Auditorium. See Map
** - Note that the October Session is the 3rd Sunday.
*** - Biplanes can be any size, but must be tissue covered. For official flights they are to R.O.G. and fly with any unmodified plastic prop.

New fliers, whether novice or skilled, are assured of help and a warm welcome so come along to fly or watch.

From May to October, with the better weather, we also fly outdoors, on the second Sunday of those months at the same location, conditions permitting.
