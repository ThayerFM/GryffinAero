Tom Whitworth's Dornier 335

Tom is justifiably proud of this plans-built Dornier 335.

He designed the camoflauge on his computer using Freehand and Photoshop before printing on the tissue. The result is stunning.

Tom usually flies the model with a single motor driving the forward propeller. We are trying to get Tom to carve a matched pair of props so that it can be flown on a single motor turning both props.
