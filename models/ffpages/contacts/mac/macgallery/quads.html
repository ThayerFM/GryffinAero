Jason McGuire's

Original No Pain Quadruplane

(and progeny!)

This delightful design is one of Jason's most recent. The larger blue model is his original 12" span prototype. After seeing it fly, I had to build the half-sized green one with its carved balsa prop (at left). Click for more pictures of the prototype in action. I also have a few bare bones shots of mine.

Fellow Marin flyers George Benson and Jerry Long have also followed suit, and we have a few pix of all 4 together.
