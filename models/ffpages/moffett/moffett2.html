Indoor Free Flight Flying Sessions

Hangar One, Moffett Field

December 8, 2000

Indoor model flying at Moffett Field has been suspended indefinitely due to a recently discovered problem with lead contamination in the floor and walls. All personnel have been moved out, including those of the Airship Museum.

It will cost approximately three million dollars to decontaminate it.
No one knows where the money will come from.

Updated information will be posted here if and when it becomes available.
