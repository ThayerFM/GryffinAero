Bellanca Light Tractor by Bruce Shepard

Bruce Shepard of Yuma, AZ built this example and writes...

I thought you might be interested in having a photo of your Bellanca I built. I am real pleased with how the model turned out and it is a great looking bipe. All in all it was a great model to build.

Weight is 8.5 oz, with 3 Tadiran Lithium Ion cells.
Motor is a DC5-2.4 geared 5:1 with a VL 7.25" prop.
