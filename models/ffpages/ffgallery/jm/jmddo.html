Jason McGuire's

Daddy-O Embryo Racer

This sport model was obviously inspired by the Golden Age racers. After flipping through a few books, Jason couldn't help but break out the paper and pencil. This exquisite structure is the result. It conforms to Embryo class rules, meeting wing area and fuselage cross section requirements.

Now nearly completed (are they ever really done?), this model has done over 2 minutes indoors.

Why yes, now that you mention it, the Daddy-O would make a great RC park flyer!

I thought the same, doubled the plans and in February 2005, flew my first Daddy-O.
It worked so well I followed it with yet another in August of 2007.
