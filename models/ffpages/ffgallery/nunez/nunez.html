Rumpler from George Nunez

A great looking Rumpler from George Nunez of Miami. It is an old Guillows 24" Rumpler outline, but with an enlarged rudder, box fuse, scale rib spacing. It flys very well and has won many meets. It weighs 37 g (about what a trailing edge from the kit, joke) and flys left on a Peck 8" and a single 27" loop of .150. Does over a minute indoors.
