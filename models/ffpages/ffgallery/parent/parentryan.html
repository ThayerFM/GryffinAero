Treasures from Chris Parent

Herr Ryan ST

Covered with JCI silver it is a slow pretty flier despite chunky 75+ gram weight. It's big at 30" and will actually roll out on landing in the New England hard ground of early April. I flew it at sunset last night; the silver looks great. I've only done short, 800 turn - 35 second flights so far. It chews up a small football field size space pretty fast.
