Flying Aces Moth

Available as a kit from Peck-Polymers, the Flying Aces Moth is a classic "stick and tissue" Old Timer design dating from 1937. With a 24" wing span and light weight, it is a great flyer, in danger of flying OOS in any passing thermal.

My model has had two distinct lives.
