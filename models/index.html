Free Flight Fantasies

Thanks for stopping by to visit my hangar. When not working, I spend much of my free time designing, building and flying miniature aircraft. They are usually either Radio Control, or smaller and lighter Free Flight models that are on their own once released to the winds. I have dabbled occasionally with Control Line. There is also a full scale airplane in my life.
