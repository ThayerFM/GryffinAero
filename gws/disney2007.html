Gryffin visits Disney and
the Kennedy Space Center

January 2007

For the second straight year, Gryffin gets to visit Disney right after his birthday. He also spend a day at the Kennedy Space Center.

Another twist this year was that Gryffin had his own digital camera to bring along. It does a great job of capturing the world through the eyes of a 4 year old. Hopefully you will be able to tell the difference between Gryffin's Fisher Price images, and Daddy's Canon DSLR.

Gryffin's camera captures his anticipation the night we arrived at the hotel. Early to bed means early to Disney.
